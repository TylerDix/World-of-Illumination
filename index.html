Plan for your trip.

We have a few tips to make your experience as enjoyable as possible.

  • Purchase online

    • Save time and money by purchasing online, in advance. We've made the process super simple: Pick your time, pick your location, then load up the car and go!


  • Make a pit stop

    • Restrooms are positioned at the front gate, the entrance to the show, and at the Holiday Boulevard Marketplace. But it's a good idea to go before you really need to go.


  • Keep the kids entertained

    • We work very hard to keep waiting times to a minimum, but demand to see the show can spike on weekends and holidays. We recommend bringing anything to keep your kiddos entertained while you make your way in to the show.


  • Bring warm clothes

    • You can enjoy the entire lightshow from the comfort of your car, but we hope you enjoy the Holiday Boulevard Marketplace after the show (North Phoenix location only). And we don't want you to get chilly.
